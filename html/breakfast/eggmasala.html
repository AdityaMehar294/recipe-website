Egg Masala

Egg Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that combines the richness of eggs with the flavors of Manchurian sauce.Egg Manchurian can be served in various forms, including dry, semi-gravy, or with a thick sauce. It pairs well with fried rice or noodles, making it a versatile dish.

Instructions:

  1. Cook Chow Mein noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside..
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan. Add onions and stir-fry until translucent. Add cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and mushrooms. Stir-fry until vegetables are slightly tender.
  3. Add the boiled noodles to the vegetables. Toss well to combine.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, chili sauce (if using), vinegar, sugar, and salt. Pour this sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together.
  5. Stir-fry on high heat for a few more minutes until everything is well combined, and the noodles get a slightly crispy texture.
  6. Garnish with chopped green onions. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  1. 200g Chow Mein noodles
  2. 1 cup shredded cabbage
  3. 1 cup julienned carrots
  4. 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (colorful)
  5. 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  6. 1/2 cup thinly sliced onions
  7. 2 tbsp soy sauce
  8. 1 tsp vinegar
  9. Chopped green onions for garnish
