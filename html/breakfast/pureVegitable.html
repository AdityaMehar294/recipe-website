Pure Vegetable Bowl

A pure vegetable bowl is a dish that consists entirely of vegetables, providing a healthy and nutritious option for those seeking a plant-based meal.Pure vegetable bowls are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, offering a diverse range of nutrients without any animal products.

Instructions:

  1. Cook quinoa or brown rice according to package instructions. Let it cool..
  2. Toss sliced zucchini, yellow squash, and red onion with olive oil, salt, pepper, and dried herbs.
  3. Spread the vegetables on a baking sheet and roast in the oven for about 20-25 minutes or until they are tender and slightly golden.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, julienned carrot, and sliced avocado.
  5. Optionally, add crumbled feta cheese or goat cheese and sprinkle nuts or seeds on top for extra flavor and crunch.
  6. Toss everything together gently or serve as is. Enjoy your nutritious and delicious Pure Vegetable Bowl!

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups cooked quinoa or brown rice
  2. Mixed greens (spinach, kale, arugula, or your choice)
  3. 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  4. 1 cucumber, sliced
  5. 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  6. 1 carrot, julienned
  7. 2 tbsp soy sauce
  8. 1 avocado, sliced
  9. Chopped green onions for garnish
