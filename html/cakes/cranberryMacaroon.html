Cranberry Macaroon

This delightful Cranberry Macaroon Ice Cream Cake combines the tartness of cranberries, the sweetness of macarons, and the creaminess of ice cream.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C).
  2. In a food processor, pulse almond flour and powdered sugar until well combined
  3. Gently fold the almond flour mixture into the egg whites until smooth.
  4. Transfer the batter to a piping bag and pipe small circles onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Let the macarons rest for 30 minutes before baking..
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until the tops are set. Let them cool completely..
  7. Spread cranberry jam on the flat side of one macaron and sandwich with another..

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup cranberries
  2. 1 cup shredded coconut
  3. 1 cup all-purpose flour.
  4. 1 cup granulated sugar
  5. 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  6. 2 large eggs
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  8. 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  9. 1/4 teaspoon salt
