Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin soup is a nutrient-rich dish, providing essential vitamins and minerals. Pumpkins are a good source of vitamin A, potassium, and fiber, contributing to overall health and well-being.

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until the onions are translucent..
  2. Add the pumpkin puree and diced potato to the pot. Stir to combine with the onions and garlic..
  3. Season the mixture with ground cinnamon, ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Stir well to evenly distribute the spices.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth, ensuring that it covers the vegetables. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil..
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender..
  6. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. If you don't have an immersion blender, carefully transfer the soup to a blender in batches.
  7. Stir in the coconut milk, adjusting the consistency as desired. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups pumpkin puree (canned or homemade)
  2. 1 large onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 medium-sized potato, peeled and diced.
  5. 4 cups vegetable broth
  6. 1 cup coconut milk.
  7. 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  8. 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Olive oil for sautéing
